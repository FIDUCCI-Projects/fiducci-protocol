# FIDUCCI | Tokenization as a Service (TaaS) 🛡️⚖️

### Institutional-Grade RWA Infrastructure on Arbitrum.

FIDUCCI is a decentralized "Trust Stack" designed to bridge high-value Real World Assets (RWA) with on-chain liquidity. By combining professional legal frameworks (**Mexican Land Trusts**) with cutting-edge Web3 technology (**Chainlink CRE** and **FHE**), we enable the fractionalization of large-scale real estate assets into accessible, liquid, and secure digital units.

---

## 🏗️ Core Architecture: The "Trust Stack"

Our infrastructure is built on four fundamental pillars to ensure institutional adoption:

| Pillar | Technology | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Legality** | Mexican Fideicomiso (Land Trust) | 1:1 Legal backing and regulatory compliance. |
| **Solidity** | Arbitrum L2 + Stylus | Scalable, low-latency, and secure settlement layer. |
| **Verifiability** | Chainlink CRE & Functions | Real-time valuation via institutional APIs (Intelimetrica/Tinsa). |
| **Privacy** | FHE (Fully Homomorphic Encryption) | Data protection for sensitive investor and asset information. |

---

## 🇲🇽 Genesis Case: Benchmarking Scale

To stress-test our TaaS engine, we are using a **$6M USD Land Development** in Puebla, Mexico, as our primary benchmark. 

- **Asset Value:** $6,000,000 USD
- **Strategy:** Fractionalizing 20 hectares into liquid tokens ($30 USD/sqm).
- **Oracle Pipeline:** Automated price feeds connecting the state's Notarial/Cadastral data with Arbitrum.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Stack & Integrations

- **Network:** [Arbitrum One](https://arbitrum.io/) (Planned migration to Stylus for high-performance compute).
- **Oracle Framework:** [Chainlink Functions](https://chainlink.re/) & Runtime Environment (CRE).
- **Data Oracles:** Real-time integration with data providers like **Intelimetrica** APIs for localized market data.
- **Smart Contracts:** Solidty-based vault and fractionalization logic.
